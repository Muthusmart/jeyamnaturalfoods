Jeyam Natural Ghee

Jeyam Natural Ghee is made with 100% organic butter from grass-fed cows on small family farms in India. Silky smooth and luscious, our Ghee is the real deal! Ghee is made by simmering butter over low heat and removing the milk solids as they form.

We take great care and consideration in the preparation and processing method. Our uncompromising commitment to quality combined with obsessive attention to detail allows for a unique natural ghee.
